Elisa Sovrano

Assistant professor (RTDB) @ UNIMORE

I am a researcher in nonlinear analysis. I work on boundary value problems associated with ordinary and partial differential equations, and on dynamical systems. My goal is to make applied models more understandable from a mathematical point of view.

Experience

Lately

I am Assistant Professor (RTdB) at UNIMORE - DSMI, Modena and Reggio Emilia (Italy)

  • Italian National Scientific Qualification (ASN) as Associate Professor, 2020-2029


Previously

I was a Postdoc Researcher at

  • UNIMORE - DSMI, Modena and Reggio Emilia (Italy). [Supervisor: Prof. Luisa Malaguti]
  • CNRS - CAMS, EHESS, Paris (France) [Supervisor: Prof. Henri Berestycki]
  • INdAM - DMG, University of Trieste (Italy) [Supervisor: Prof. Pierpaolo Omari]
  • CMUP, Porto (Portugal) [Supervisor: Prof. Isabel S. Labouriau]

I was also a Visiting Researcher at

  • CMAF-CIO, University of Lisbon (Portugal)

I got a PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics and Physics at

  • University of Udine (Italy) [Advisor: Prof. Fabio Zanolin]

Research

Ongoing projects

  • Boundary value problems with indefinite weights
  • System of coupled equations with degenerate diffusion
  • Reaction-convection-saturated diffusion models
  • Reaction-diffusion models with transmission conditions
  • Discrete-time population models for threshold harvesting
